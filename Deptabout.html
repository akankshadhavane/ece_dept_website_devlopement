About Us

Welcome to ECE Department

VISION

Promote effective teaching learning in the fields of electronics and computer engineering to produce nationally, globally competitive engineers that are needed to meet the evolving demands of industry and society.

MISSION
  • M1- To impart the latest multidisciplinary technical knowledge in the field of Electronics and Computer Engineering
  • M2- To promote innovative ideas for solving multidisciplinary engineering problems and provide opportunity for creating talent in students to take up challenges for the benefits of society and industry.
  • M3- To promote collaboration between industry and academia for the benefit of students' overall development.
GOAL SHORT TERM GOAL
  • To impart quality technical education in the area of Electronics and Computer Engineering in order to meet the challenges of industry and society.
  • To establish the center of excellence in the emerging areas of Electronics and Computer Engineering.
  • To encourage the students to become entrepreneurs.
LONG TERM GOAL
  • To provide better placement opportunities to the students.
  • To train the students in soft skills, Aptitude, and motivate for competitive examinations.
  • To organize various events and short-term courses in the area of Electronics and Computer Engineering.
  • To update the better educational environment and focus on industrial collaboration.
OUR SPECIALITY

We Have

Advanced Labs

Our Department have advanced laboratories equipped with the latest technologies. Our advanced facilities provide students with a hands-on learning experience, fostering creativity and innovation.

Innovative Ideas Incubator

we believe in nurturing the spirit of innovation. Our labs serve as incubators for groundbreaking ideas, where students are encouraged to explore, experiment, and develop solutions to real-world challenges.

Industry-Relevant Software Development

Our Computer Science curriculum is designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills in software development. From algorithmic design to software engineering principles, our courses prepare students for success in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Faculty Expertise in Emerging Technologies

Our faculty members in Electronics and Computer are experts in their fields, specializing in emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), cybersecurity, and blockchain. Their expertise ensures that students receive up-to-date and relevant knowledge.

Tech Talks and Guest Lectures

Enrich your learning experience with regular tech talks and guest lectures featuring industry professionals and experts. These events provide valuable insights into the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities in the fields of Electronics and Computer.

Hackathons and Coding Competitions

Join our Computer Science enthusiasts in hackathons and coding competitions, where students showcase their coding prowess and problem-solving skills. These events provide a platform for networking and exposure to the competitive tech industry

Modern and Spacious Learning Spaces

Step into our classrooms, where modern design meets comfort. Our spacious classrooms provide an ideal setting for collaborative learning, ensuring that students have ample room to engage in discussions and group activities.

Innovative Teaching Tools

Explore a range of innovative teaching tools integrated into our classrooms. From virtual labs to simulation software, these tools enhance the learning experience and provide students with practical insights into their fields of study.
